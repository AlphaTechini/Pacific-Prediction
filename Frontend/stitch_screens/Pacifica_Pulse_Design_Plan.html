# Pacifica Pulse - Prediction Market Platform PRD ## Project Overview Pacifica Pulse is a high-fidelity prediction market overlay for perpetual trading platforms. It allows traders to participate in short-term markets based on real-time market signals (price, volume, funding, etc.). ## Visual Identity: "The Kinetic Terminal" - **Color Palette:** Deep blacks and obsidian grays with neon cyan/electric blue accents (#00F0FF). - **Typography:** Space Grotesk for a modern, technical feel. - **Vibe:** Serious, fast, information-dense, but clean and minimalist. ## Proposed Screens ### 1. Dashboard (The Command Center) The main hub for users. - **Features:** A high-level overview of the most active and trending markets, a summary of the user's active positions and portfolio performance, and a live "Pulse" feed of real-time market signal changes. - **Purpose:** To give users an immediate sense of the market's "heartbeat" and quick access to their most important data. ### 2. Markets (The Discovery Engine) A comprehensive, filterable list of all available prediction markets. - **Features:** Categorized by signal type (Price, Funding, Volume), time to expiry, and volume. Uses compact "Market Cards" with mini-charts, countdown timers, and quick "Yes/No" interaction points. - **Purpose:** Efficient discovery and comparison of different trading opportunities. ### 3. Market Details (The Deep Dive) A focused view of a single prediction market. - **Features:** Detailed market signal chart, order book/depth (if applicable), AI-generated market insight summary, participant sentiment analysis, and a dedicated trading panel for taking positions. - **Purpose:** Providing all the technical intelligence a trader needs to make a high-confidence prediction. ### 4. Create Market (The Signal Forge) A clean, form-driven interface for users to propose or launch new markets. - **Features:** Intuitive step-by-step selection of the underlying signal, target threshold, expiry time, and initial liquidity. - **Purpose:** Empowering users to define their own market hypotheses based on technical indicators. ### 5. Portfolio (The Performance Ledger) A detailed breakdown of the user's history and active stakes. - **Features:** Performance metrics (PNL, Win Rate, Streaks), detailed history of closed positions, and management tools for active positions. - **Purpose:** Helping traders track their success and manage their risk over time. ### 6. Leaderboard (The Alpha Ranking) A competitive view of the top-performing traders. - **Features:** Rankings by PNL, win rate, and total volume. Profiles with performance badges and "top signal" tags. - **Purpose:** Gamification and identifying "Alpha" traders for social proof.
